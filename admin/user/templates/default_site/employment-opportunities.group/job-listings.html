Current Openings

Classified, Full-Time Faculty, Wage and CCWA Positions

John Tyler Community College only accepts online applications submitted through the Virginia Jobs web site for classified, full-time faculty, wage (hourly) and CCWA positions. We are unable to accept applications by any other method of submission. If you need computer access to submit your application, visit the Virginia Employment Commission or your local public library. 

All applications will be screened according to the position requirements. Only individuals selected for an interview will be contacted. You should periodically check the Virginia Jobs website to review the status of your application.
