Jumpstarting Limitless Opportunity

Engineering grad and physician-to-be Alex Ritchie views medicine from multiple angles. After a NASA internship, leading campus groups, and two years at Tyler, she’s completing her bachelor’s in biomedical engineering at Virginia Commonwealth University—and winning awards for investigating how to use nanoparticles to regenerate lung tissue.

Training for Advancement

Joe Dettmer had the opportunity to move into a new role at work. the only catch? It required electrical skills he didn’t have. To get up to speed, Joe took classes at Tyler and now has the right skills and qualifications to move forward in today’s fast-paced workplace, all while staying at the company he’s been with for decades.
